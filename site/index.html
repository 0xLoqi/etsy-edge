Frequently asked questions

Is Tag Spy really free?

Yes, 100% free with no limits. You can see the tags on any Etsy listing without paying anything or even creating an account. Tag Spy and the basic SEO score will be free forever.

How does Etsy Edge see the tags?

Etsy includes listing tags in the page's structured data (JSON-LD) and through the Etsy Open API. This is publicly available data ‚Äî we just surface it in a convenient overlay while you browse.

Does it work on all Etsy listings?

Yes. Etsy Edge works on every public Etsy listing page. Just navigate to any listing and the panel appears automatically in the bottom-right corner.

How are AI tag suggestions generated?

AI suggestions use GPT-4o-mini, trained on Etsy SEO best practices. It analyzes your listing's title, description, and what top competitors are using, then generates 13 optimized tags with reasoning for each one.

Is my data safe?

Yes. We don't collect, store, or log any personal data. API calls are routed through our secure backend server (Cloudflare Workers) so you never need to manage API keys ‚Äî but no request data is stored or logged on our end. Cached results stay local in your browser. Read our privacy policy for details.

Can I cancel Pro anytime?

Yes, cancel anytime with one click. No long-term contracts. Your Pro features stay active until the end of your billing period, and Tag Spy remains free forever.

Why not just use eRank or Marmalead?

Those are great tools, but they require you to leave Etsy and use a separate dashboard. Etsy Edge works right on the listing page ‚Äî no context switching. Plus, Tag Spy is free (eRank charges for tag viewing), and our AI suggestions are unique to Etsy Edge.
